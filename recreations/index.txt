## Dracula

Count Dracula had directed me to go to the Golden Krone Hotel, which I found, to my delight, to be thoroughly old-fashioned, for of course I wanted to see all I could of the ways of the country. I was evidently expected, for when I got near the door I faced a cheery-looking elderly woman in the usual peasant dress—white undergarment with long double apron, front and back, of coloured stuff fitting almost too tight for modesty. When I came close she bowed, and said: “The Herr Englishman?” “Yes,” I said, “Jonathan Harker.” She smiled, and gave some message to an elderly man in white shirt-sleeves, who had followed her to the door. He went, but immediately returned with a letter:—

# 電子書籍の標準形式について

2020年現在、電子書籍の配布は epub が標準的な形式です。epub 形式については「今更だけどEPUB（イーパブ）ファイルどうやって読むの？」 https://www.appleach.co.jp/2017/04/210/ というサイトの解説を参照してください。Linux のデスクトップ環境では、Atril Document Viewer で読むことが出来ます。Evince は2020年時点で未対応で、下記バグ報告を読んだ印象では今後も対応される見込みがないと思われます。

"Evince doesn't support EPUB, and the request for support has been marked as WONTFIX (see https://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=539347). However, Atril, which was originally forked from Evince, does support EPUB rather well." -- https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=20392810

GNOME デスクトップ向けとして、gnome-books が代替アプリとして開発済みです。ただし、このアプリを利用するには多数の GNOME 関連ライブラリーのインストールが必要です。EPUB ビューアはこの他に Bookworm や Calibre もあります。

ebook reader for GNOME - "Books is a simple application to access and organize your e-books on GNOME. It is meant to be a simple and elegant replacement for using a file manager to deal with e-books. It's included in the default set of core applications since GNOME 3.16."
https://wiki.gnome.org/Apps/Books
https://packages.debian.org/buster/gnome-books
